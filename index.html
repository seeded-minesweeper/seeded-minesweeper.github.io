Presets

Presets are an easy way to play custom games without having to input the game's properties (rows, columns, mines and seed) every single time. To create a preset, input the games properties in the appropriate fields and then click the save button. If the game is valid (has row & column values greater than 0 and the number of squares in the game are greater than the number of mines), the save button will be enabled. Then input the name of the preset and save it. You can now use the preset by clicking the Presets Button and selecting the preset by its name in the dropdown.
To delete a preset right click the preset in the dropdown. There arw 3 default presets already added - Easy, Medium and Hard.

Full Seeds

A Full Seed is a string of characters that has both the game's seed and other of the game's properties combined. To create a Full Seed, input the properties of the game in their respective fields and click the Full Seed Mode button, the seed will appear in the Full Seed input field. Do this the opposite way and get the game's properties from the Full Seed.

About Game

Minesweeper with seed functionality and more control over game creation.

How To Play

  • Double click or double tap on a square to open or chord it.
  • Right click on a square or tap and hold it (on touch controls) to flag or unflag it.
  • To toggle zoom states (zoom in, zoom out or no zooming) press the Control Key. Pinch in or out to zoom in or out on touch devices.
  • Click and drag the mouse to move the game's view area around. Scroll through the game area to move the game's view on touch devices.
